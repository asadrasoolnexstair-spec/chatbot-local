Working with Nexstair follows eight steps from initial contact to ongoing growth:

**Step 1 — Discovery & Strategy Consultation.** Nexstair starts by understanding the client's business goals, challenges, and budget, then maps out a clear, honest strategy tailored to the client's specific needs, whether that is web design, web development, SEO, or AI automation.

**Step 2 — Proposal & Agreement.** Within 24–48 hours, Nexstair delivers a detailed proposal covering scope, timeline, and transparent pricing, with everything agreed upfront and no hidden costs.

**Step 3 — Design Phase.** Nexstair's designers create an initial concept based on the approved brief. Clients get unlimited free revisions until they are completely satisfied before the project moves forward.

**Step 4 — Development & Build.** Nexstair builds the project on a secure test server using clean, performance-optimised code. For AI automation projects, this is where AI agents and n8n workflows are configured.

**Step 5 — SEO & AI Optimisation.** Before going live, Nexstair embeds full technical SEO, WordPress SEO configuration, schema markup, and AI-powered optimisation so the site is built to rank from day one.

**Step 6 — Testing & Quality Assurance.** Nexstair runs thorough cross-device testing, performance checks, and security audits. AI automation workflows are tested end-to-end across every trigger and output before sign-off.

**Step 7 — Launch & Handover.** Nexstair deploys to the client's live server and transfers full ownership of all code, content, and automation systems to the client.

**Step 8 — Ongoing Support & Growth.** Nexstair stays with clients after launch, providing updates, new features, SEO improvements, and automation expansions at transparent, affordable rates as a long-term digital growth partner.
