# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What services do you offer?
We offer three main types of services:
1. **Consultation Services** - Expert advice and strategic planning for your business needs
2. **Demo Sessions** - Hands-on demonstrations of our solutions
3. **Support Services** - Technical and business support to help you succeed

### How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment in several ways:
- Use our website chatbot to schedule instantly
- Call us at (555) 123-4567
- Email appointments@example.com
- Visit our office during business hours

Our business hours are:
- Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

## Booking Questions

### How far in advance can I book?
You can book appointments up to 90 days in advance. Same-day appointments may be available depending on availability.

### Can I reschedule my appointment?
Yes, you can reschedule your appointment at any time. We recommend giving at least 24 hours notice. You can:
- Use the chatbot and provide your booking reference
- Call us at (555) 123-4567
- Email appointments@example.com

### What is my booking reference number?
Your booking reference number is sent to you via email when you complete a booking. It looks like BK-XXXX-XXXX (e.g., BK-2024-1234). Keep this number handy for any changes or inquiries.

### How do I cancel my appointment?
To cancel your appointment:
1. Use the chatbot with your booking reference
2. Call us at (555) 123-4567
3. Email cancellations@example.com

Cancellations made at least 24 hours in advance are free. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.

## Payment Questions

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfers
- Cash (in-person only)

### Is there a cancellation fee?
Cancellations made 24 hours or more before your appointment are free. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee of up to 50% of the service cost.

### Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer full refunds for services that do not meet your expectations. Please contact us within 7 days of your service to request a refund.

## Technical Questions

### What should I bring to my appointment?
For most appointments, just bring yourself! For consultations, it's helpful to bring:
- Any relevant documents or data
- A list of questions or topics you'd like to discuss
- Information about your current challenges or goals

### Can I attend virtually?
Yes, many of our services can be conducted virtually via video call. When booking, you can specify your preference for in-person or virtual meetings.

### What happens after my appointment?
After your appointment, you will receive:
- A summary of what was discussed
- Any recommended next steps
- Follow-up materials or resources
- Information about how to book additional services if needed
